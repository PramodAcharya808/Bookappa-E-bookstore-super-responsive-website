shiva kumar

We often feel that fictional characters are so good that this is only possible in a book or on screen. But in reality, most people are not so moral or perfect. At some point, people make mistakes and their judgement will be clouded by emotions like jealousy, rage or resentment. Yellowface is a story about Juniper Hayward and Athena Liu.

prajwala

One of my favourite features about this book is the narration. The author has described the protagonist's thoughts elaborately and in an unrefined manner. We can see how June tries to debate with herself to reason her jealousy.

Thyagaraj

jaya

I rarely read fictions, but this book is a great read. The plot is thrilling, the prose flows naturally and shows distinctive craftsmanship. The more you read the more hooked to the story you become, so you want to find out how it ends.

kumar HKR

naveen

This is the story about an author named June Hayward and her friend, Athena Liu. They are both new authors but whereas Athena's debut becomes a bestseller, June's debut is a flop. The book starts with Athena dying in front of June's eyes. June also steals Athena's newest manuscript that she was working on about Chinese labourers during World War I.
